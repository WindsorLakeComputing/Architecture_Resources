The Point-of-View (POV) mad lib summarizes business goals and other stakeholder needs in a memorable, engaging format.
The impact of each mad lib should be outcome focused. Try the 5 Whys technique to help get to the bottom of stakeholders’ real needs: 
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/5_Whys

Design hills describe the impact stakeholders hope the software will have on 
end users. Like other ways of specifying business goals, hills try to describe 
the value the software provides, not how the software is to be built:
https://web.archive.org/web/20160123222757/http://www.ibm.com/design/thinking/keys/hills/
